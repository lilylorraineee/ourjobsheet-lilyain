About

Dato’ Siti Nurhaliza is a Malaysian singer, songwriter, and businesswoman. She is widely regarded as one of the most successful and influential artists in Southeast Asia.

Dato’ Siti Nurhaliza has won hundreds of awards throughout her career and has represented Malaysia on various international stages.

Dato’ Siti Nurhaliza is also known for her philanthropic work and involvement in charitable activities.
